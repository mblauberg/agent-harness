
## Binding-current authority

### A05-current

The Spec 05 1.14 freeze candidate consumes the closed Spec 01
capability-compiled authority contract without creating a Console-owned codec.
This W017 restatement does not broaden the shared header: until chaired
acceptance, authority remains v1.0 at `c2fc623` plus directly traced human
clarifications. Certifying review remains strictly
`review-readonly`; the Console may display, but never select, widen or silently
relabel, requested or effective authority profiles. The
`workspace-write-offline` affordance remains unavailable until its exact
provider tuple passes the Step-3 containment gate. The current status,
authority boundary and direct-human clarification trace remain those declared
in the shared authority header.

### A05

The product has one current Console, protocol and schema epoch. Old-daemon
retry, vintage-wire presentation and implicit legacy-state import are not
product requirements; incompatible state is preserved and rejected explicitly.
Current optional-feature negotiation and adapter verification remain required.
The project-scoped Console retains its project client, auto-selects only one
attachable session and otherwise uses exact session-labelled run projections
plus an explicit selector. `run-session-projection.v1` is required; a peer
without exact run/session identity is incompatible.
